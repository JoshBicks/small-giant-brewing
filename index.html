OUR BEERS

At Small Giant Brewing Co., each beer tells a story — bold, characterful, and brewed with purpose. From rich stouts to hop-forward IPAs, our small-batch creations are crafted to leave a giant impression.

DRAGON STOUT 9.7%

Dark as a Belfast winter and twice as fierce, this imperial stout roars with roasted malt, dark chocolate, and a smoky whisper of espresso.

THE GIANT IPA 5.6%

This flagship IPA balances West Coast pine with juicy citrus for a clean, bold finish. Brewed in honour of Belfast's giant spirit.

THE ZILLA IPA 8.2%

Bursting with berry, mango, and dank resin, this hazy beast is smooth, surreal, and unforgettable.

ABOUT US

In a quiet flat tucked behind Belfast’s Cathedral Quarter, Leo Small was brewing dreams long before he brewed his first pint. From a teacher to a passionate brewer, Leo's journey led to the birth of Small Giant Brewing Co. — a name inspired by the idea that even the smallest people and ideas can create something extraordinary. Now, Small Giant is a community hub for storytellers, songwriters, wanderers, and lovers of damn good beer. What began as a late-night hobby with clinking bottles and scribbled recipes soon evolved into a craft rooted in purpose. Leo didn’t just want to make beer—he wanted to make beer that meant something. Each batch pays tribute to Belfast’s grit and warmth, brewed with intention and a touch of rebellion. From the first pour to the last drop, Small Giant isn’t just about what’s in the glass—it’s about the conversations, the connections, and the stories that rise with the foam.
